# Lie Bracket

*Source lecture(s):* [PHY622 Lec10](../lectures.md)

## Physical Meaning

Measures non-commutativity of infinitesimal generators.

## Variable Definitions

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| $T_a$ | generators |
| $f_{abc}$ | structure constants |

## Assumptions

Smooth manifold, flows near identity.

## Derivation

Consider $[X,Y]=XY-YX$; at identity the commutator of vector fields yields the Lie bracket.

## Applications

Quantum mechanics (commutators), gauge theory, robotics.
