# Cauchy-Riemann Equations

*Source lecture(s):* [PHY622 Lec3](../lectures.md)

## Physical Meaning

Necessary and sufficient conditions for complex differentiability.

## Variable Definitions

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| $u$ | real part |
| $v$ | imaginary part |

## Assumptions

$u,v$ have continuous partial derivatives.

## Derivation

Equate limits along real and imaginary axes in $(f(z+\Delta z)-f(z))/\Delta z$.

## Applications

Testing analyticity, fluid dynamics (potential flow), electrostatics.
