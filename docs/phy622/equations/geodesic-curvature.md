# Geodesic Equation

*Source lecture(s):* [PHY622 Lec1](../lectures.md)

## Physical Meaning

Locally shortest/longest curves on a curved manifold.

## Variable Definitions

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| $\Gamma$ | Christoffel symbols |
| $x^i$ | generalized coordinates |

## Assumptions

Metric-compatible, torsion-free connection.

## Derivation

Apply EL to arc-length functional in curvilinear coordinates.

## Applications

General relativity (null geodesics), computer graphics (shortest paths on meshes).
