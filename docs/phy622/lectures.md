!!! tip "Where the arcs lead"
    Variational calculus becomes Lagrangian mechanics and field theory; contour
    integration powers every Green's function and dispersion relation (see
    [Landau damping](../plasma/concepts/landau-damping.md) for a spectacular physics
    application of contour deformation); group theory is the grammar of quantum
    mechanics and particle physics.
