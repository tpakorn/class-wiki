# Quizzes

## Conceptual Questions

1. What is the physical difference between Debye shielding in a plasma and
   screening in a metal?
2. Why does the frozen-in theorem fail in Sweet–Parker reconnection even though
   the plasma is still highly conducting?
3. How does the magnetic moment conservation explain the loss-cone feature in
   the Earth’s radiation belts?

## Computational Questions

1. Derive the growth rate of the Buneman instability from the two-stream
   dispersion relation.
2. Calculate the Lundquist number for a tokamak plasma with $n=10^{20}$ m$^{-3}$,
   $T=1$ keV, $B=3$ T, $L=10$ m, $\eta=10^{-6}$ m$^2$/s.
3. For a Z-pinch with $I=1$ MA and $a=0.5$ m, compute the on-axis $B_\theta$
   and the plasma $\beta$ if $p=2$ Pa.

## Multiple-Choice Questions

1. **Plasma Frequency**
   - A) $\omega_{pe} \propto \sqrt{n_e}$
   - B) $\omega_{pe} \propto n_e$
   - C) $\omega_{pe} \propto \sqrt{T_e}$
   - D) $\omega_{pe} \propto T_e$
   > **Answer:** A

2. **Debye Length**
   - A) Grows with temperature
   - B) Shrinks with density
   - C) Is independent of charge
   - D) Both A and B
   > **Answer:** D

3. **Frozen-in Theorem**
   - A) Always true in real plasmas
   - B) True when $\eta = 0$
   - C) Requires $T_i = T_e$
   - D) Applies only to unmagnetized flows
   > **Answer:** B

4. **Sweet–Parker Rate**
   - A) $M_A \sim S^{1/2}$
   - B) $M_A \sim 1/S$
   - C) $M_A \sim S^{-1/2}$
   - D) $M_A \sim$ constant
   > **Answer:** C

5. **Landau Damping**
   - A) Needs Coulomb collisions
   - B) Is strongest when $k\lambda_D \gg 1$
   - C) Depends on $\partial f_0/\partial v$ at resonance
   - D) Only occurs for ions
   > **Answer:** C
