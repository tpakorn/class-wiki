# Laplace Transform

*Source lecture(s):* [PHY621 Lec4](../lectures.md)

## Physical Meaning

Converts time-domain functions to complex frequency $s$-plane representations.

## Variable Definitions

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| $s$ | complex frequency |
| $f(t)$ | time-domain function |

## Assumptions

$f(t)=0$ for $t<0$ (unilateral); region of convergence required.

## Derivation

Direct from integral definition; table entries follow by integration-by-parts.

## Applications

RC/RL/RLC circuits, control theory, initial-value problems.

## Connections to Other Equations

- fourier-integral
- convolution
