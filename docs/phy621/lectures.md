!!! tip "The spine of the course"
    Everything converges on solving physics PDEs:
    [linear algebra](concepts/eigenvalues-eigenvectors.md) supplies the eigenmode
    language, [transforms](concepts/fourier-transform.md) diagonalize derivatives, and
    [Green's functions](concepts/greens-functions.md) assemble the general solution
    from point sources. PHY622 continues with
    [calculus of variations, complex analysis and group theory](../phy622/lectures.md).
