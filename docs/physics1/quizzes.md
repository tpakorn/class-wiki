# Quizzes

## Conceptual Questions

Q1: If net external torque on a system is zero, what is conserved?
A: Angular momentum.

Q2: A block slides at constant speed. What is the net horizontal force?
A: Zero.

Q3: Why does a boat float?
A: Average density of the ship plus trapped air is less than water density.

## Computational Problems

Q1: A $0.5\,\text{kg}$ block on a $30°$ incline requires what parallel force to hold it?
A: $0.5\;g\sin30° = 2.45\,\text{N}$.

Q2: Wave speed on a string with $\mu = 0.02\,\text{kg/m}$ under tension $T=200\,\text{N}$.
A: $v = \sqrt{T/\mu} \approx 100\,\text{m/s}$.

## Multiple-Choice

Q1: Which of the following increases the range of a projectile?
A) Higher launch speed
B) Lower launch angle for all cases
C) Higher altitude only
D) Lower gravity
Answer: A

Q2: For an ideal gas at constant volume, doubling temperature does what to pressure?
A) Nothing
B) Halves it
C) Doubles it
D) Quadruples it
Answer: C
