# Projectile Playground

## Learning goal

Feel how [projectile motion](../concepts/projectile-motion.md) responds to its two
inputs: range peaks at 45°, complementary angles land together, and no shot ever
escapes the **parabola of safety**.

<iframe src="../../assets/projectile.html" width="100%" height="580"
        style="border:none;" title="Projectile playground"></iframe>

## Things to try

1. **Fire 30° and 60°** at the same speed — same range, different flight time. Which
   arrives first, and why?
2. **Sweep the angle** at fixed speed and watch the ranges trace out a maximum at 45°.
3. **Turn on the parabola of safety** and try to land a shot beyond it. (You can't —
   the envelope is $y = v_0^2/2g - gx^2/2v_0^2$.)
4. **Double the speed** and confirm the range quadruples ($R \propto v_0^2$).
