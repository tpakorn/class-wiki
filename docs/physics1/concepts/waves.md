# Waves

Waves transport energy and momentum without permanent transport of matter.

## Intuition
A stadium wave is people standing and sitting — the disturbance moves, not the people.

## Formal Definition
Wave equation for a string:
$$
\frac{\partial^2 y}{\partial t^2} = v^2 \frac{\partial^2 y}{\partial x^2}
$$

## Worked Example
Wave speed on a string depends on tension and linear density.

## Common Mistakes
Confusing wave speed with particle speed.

Q: What travels in a wave?
A: Energy and phase — not the medium permanently.
