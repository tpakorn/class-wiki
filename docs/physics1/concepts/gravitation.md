# Gravitation

Gravity acts between every pair of masses.

## Intuition
The same gravity that pulls an apple to the ground keeps the Moon in orbit.

## Formal Definition
Newton’s law:
$$
F = G\frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2}
$$

Uniform circular orbit:
$$
\frac{G m M}{r^2} = \frac{m v^2}{r}
$$

## Worked Example
Low Earth orbit speed ~ $7.9\,\text{km/s}$ from equating gravity to centripetal force.

## Common Mistakes
Using surface formula $g$ at satellite altitudes instead of $GM/r^2$.
