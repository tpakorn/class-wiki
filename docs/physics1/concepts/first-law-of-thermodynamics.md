# First Law of Thermodynamics

The first law states that energy is conserved in thermal processes.

## Intuition
Compressing air in a bicycle pump makes the pump hot — work becomes internal energy.

## Formal Definition
$$
\Delta U = Q - W
$$

## Worked Example
Gas absorbs $500\,\text{J}$ heat and does $200\,\text{J}$ expansion work; $\Delta U = 300\,\text{J}$.
