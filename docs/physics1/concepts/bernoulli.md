# Bernoulli

Bernoulli’s equation links pressure, speed, and height along a steady, inviscid streamtube.

## Intuition
Fast-moving streamlines have lower pressure — how a spray bottle works.

## Formal Definition
$$
P + \tfrac12\rho v^2 + \rho g y = \text{constant}
$$

## Worked Example
Flow through a narrowed pipe speeds up and drops in pressure.

## Common Mistakes
Applying it across viscous regions or with energy sources in between.

## Quiz Questions
Q: In horizontal steady flow, what happens to pressure when speed increases?
A: It decreases.
