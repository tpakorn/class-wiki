# Potential Energy and Conservation of Energy

Potential energy stores energy due to position or configuration.

## Intuition
Lifting a book stores gravitational energy that can later turn into motion.

## Formal Definition
Gravitational PE near Earth:
$$
U_g = mgh
$$

Elastic PE:
$$
U_s = \tfrac12 k x^2
$$

 Conservation: $E = K + U = \text{constant}$ for conservative forces.

## Worked Example
A $2\,\text{kg}$ book $2\,\text{m}$ above floor has $U_g = 39.2\,\text{J}$.

## Common Mistakes
Adding non-conservative work without accounting for losses.

Q: In free fall, what is conserved if air resistance is negligible?
A: Mechanical energy $K+U$.
