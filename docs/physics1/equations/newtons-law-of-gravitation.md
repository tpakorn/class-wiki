# Newton’s Law of Gravitation


Gravitational force between two point masses.

$$
F = G\frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2}
$$

**Variable table**

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| $G$ | gravitational constant |
| $r$ | separation |

**Applications**
- Orbits, tides, free-fall near Earth
