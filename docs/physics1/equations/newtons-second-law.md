# Newton’s Second Law


Net force equals mass times acceleration.

$$
\vec{F}_{net} = m\vec{a}
$$

**Assumptions**
- Inertial reference frame
- Mass constant

**Applications**
- Predicting motion
- Rocket thrust
- Structural loads
