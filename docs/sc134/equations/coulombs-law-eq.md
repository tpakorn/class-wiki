# Coulomb's Law

*Source lecture(s):* [SC134 Lec1-2](../lectures.md)

## Physical Meaning

Electrostatic force between point charges.

## Variable Definitions

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| $q_1,q_2$ | point charges |
| $r$ | separation |
| $\varepsilon_0$ | permittivity of free space |

## Assumptions

Point charges or spherically symmetric distributions; vacuum.

## Derivation

Experimental law from torsion-balance measurements.

## Applications

Molecular forces, Millikan oil drop, electrostatic precipitators.
