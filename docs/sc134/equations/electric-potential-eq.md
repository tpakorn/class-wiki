# Electric Potential

*Source lecture(s):* [SC134 Lec5](../lectures.md)

## Physical Meaning

Potential energy per unit charge.

## Variable Definitions

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| $V$ | potential |
| $U$ | potential energy |
| $q$ | charge |

## Assumptions

Conservative electrostatic field; zero at infinity.

## Derivation

Work to bring $q$ from infinity against conservative force, divide by $q$.

## Applications

Voltmeters, capacitor design, nerve-cell potentials.
